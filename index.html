Friends of IIT Jodhpur

IIT Jodhpur, is organizing the Friends of IIT Jodhpur Conclave on 11-12 February 2023. The conclave invites the well-wishers, Pan-IIT Alumni, Corporates, foundations, CSR representatives to participate enriching discussion sessions, plenary talks, exhibition of various impactful projects of IIT Jodhpur and other social events.
We extend cordial invitation to attend the conclave

Jodhpur City

Being a place of princely palaces, magnificent forts as well as age old temples, this city of Rajasthan is among the famous tourist destinations in the state as well as India.

This city basks in the golden history and heritage of the state and is located at the fringe of the great Thar Desert. The city was founded in 1459 A.D by the Rathore rulers.

Jodhpur is a colourful city. It throbs with beauty, glory and warmth of a populous desert city. The traditional folk dance and music performances, which are held at night, are great attractions to the visitors.
